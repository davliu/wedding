Things to do in LA
Anything you want, but if you want to have an awesome day, do this:
  • Wake up and get to Savoy by 11am. Order a hainan chicken rice (regular) and a lemon ice tea. If you feel extra hungry, order shrimp rolls.
  • Go to Fosselman's. Get a single scoop of taro ice cream. Try to not get any other flavors. Sugar cone is fine.
  • Drive to The Broad. Walk around, chill, and take some pictures. Be sure to take a picture with the Michael Jackson statue.
  • Drive to Santa Monica and park at the library because it has cheaper rates.
  • Walk to Wilshire and then walk towards the beach. When you reach 3rd street, walk down and explore it until you reach the mall. Most performers are pretty cool, but this guy will blow your mind.
  • Once you reach the mall, go towards the pier. Open your Pokemon Go app and play with some of the last remaining players in SoCal. Ride the ferris wheel. If you're traveling with somebody, hold their hand and share a romantic kiss.
  • Drive to Sawtelle and park in the Petsmart plaza for free.
  • Walk to Tsujita LA. Don't mistakenly go to Tsujita Annex or Killer Noodle. Get the Cha Siu Tsukemen and follow the eating instructions on their menu STEP BY STEP. Savor the egg.
  • Walk to B Sweet. Get a small order of bread pudding with ice cream.
  • Go home and relax.
